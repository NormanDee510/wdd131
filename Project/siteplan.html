Site Plan

Site Name

Green Trails Hiking Club

This website is dedicated to hiking enthusiasts who love to explore scenic trails and connect with nature.

Optional domain availability: greentrailsclub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about popular hiking trails, organize group hikes, and offer resources on hiking safety and gear recommendations.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking trails for beginners in my area?
  • How can I join a group hike with experienced guides?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Blue (#3498db) - Used for headings and buttons

Accent Color: Red (#e74c3c) - Used for call-to-action buttons and important alerts

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (Bold) - Used for all major headings

Body Font: Open Sans (Regular) - Used for general content
